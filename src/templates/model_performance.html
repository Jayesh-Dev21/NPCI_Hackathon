Hybrid Ensemble Strategy

Our novel ensemble combines XGBoost's high precision (93.8%) with LightGBM's architectural strengths through weighted probability averaging. This hybrid approach minimizes false positives while maintaining strong recall.
Ensemble_Prob = 0.55 × XGBoost_Prob + 0.45 × LightGBM_Prob

Precision Optimization

XGBoost contributes 93.8% precision, minimizing false alarms that plague production SOC teams. Critical for enterprise adoption.

Complementary Strengths

LightGBM provides architectural diversity and fast inference. Different tree-building strategies reduce overfitting to training patterns.

Threshold Tuning

Optimal threshold of 0.05 identified through grid search on validation set, balancing detection rate with FPR constraints.
